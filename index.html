What is The CUNY Crypto Club?

The CUNY Crypto Club at Baruch College is a student-run organization that aims to serve as a central resource for education about Blockchain Technology and Cryptocurrencies.

Our goal Establish a space for blockchain and cryptocurrency education and research at Baruch.Provide students with the tools to become fluent in blockchain technology and distributed application development though, speakers, educational material, workshops, challenges, and real world projects.

Next

Why does CryptoCurrency and Blockchain Matter?

Cryptocurrency challenges modern finance and economics, by taking middlemen (governments and banks) out of the monetary supply.

Blockchain Technology, creates a secure, immutable ledger, that takes out errors, middlemen, and fraud from complex systems efficiently. Any complex organizational system / industry is likely to adopt elements of Blockchain in the upcoming decades. Walmart, Government, Real Estate, Healthcare, Supply Chain
